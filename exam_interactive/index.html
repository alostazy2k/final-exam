Question 1: Choose the only correct answer from the following:
  1. The solution set of the inequality \( 4x + 3 < 11 \) is:
    1. \( x < 11 \)
    2. \( (-\infty, 2) \)
    3. \( [2, \infty) \)
    4. \( x > 4 \)
  2. Given \( f(x) = 4x + 10 \) and \( g(x) = \sqrt{x+2} \), find \( a \) such that \( f(g(a)) = 10 \):
    1. \( 8 \)
    2. \( 4 \)
    3. \( 0 \)
    4. \( -2 \)
  3. Given \( \displaystyle \lim_{x\to1} \frac{ax^2+bx-5}{x-1}=7 \), the values of \( a,b \):
    1. \( a=1,b=6 \)
    2. \( a=4,b=-2 \)
    3. \( a=2,b=3 \)
    4. \( a=7,b=0 \)
  4. If \( \sin(xy)=\frac12 \), then \( \frac{dx}{dy} = \):
    1. \( -\frac{y}{x} \)
    2. \( \frac{y}{x} \)
    3. \( -\frac{x}{y} \)
    4. \( \frac{x}{y} \)
  5. If \( y = -\frac{1}{2x} \), then \( y'' = \):
    1. \( -\frac{1}{x^2} \)
    2. \( \frac{1}{x^3} \)
    3. \( \frac{1}{x^2} \)
    4. \( -\frac{1}{x^3} \)
  6. Let \( f(x)=x^3-3x \). How many critical numbers?
    1. \( -1 \)
    2. \( 3 \)
    3. \( 2 \)
    4. \( 1 \)
  7. The absolute maximum of \( f(x)=x^2+2 \) on \( [-1,2] \) is:
    1. \( 6 \)
    2. \( 2 \)
    3. \( -3 \)
    4. \( 4 \)
  8. If \( y = \sin^{-1}(2x) \), then \( \frac{dy}{dx} = \):
    1. \( \frac{1}{1+4x^2} \)
    2. \( \frac{2}{\sqrt{1-4x^2}} \)
    3. \( \frac{2}{1+4x^2} \)
    4. \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)

Question 3: Evaluate each of the following limits (if exist)
  1. \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x}{\pi} \)
  2. \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{(3+x)^2 - 9}{x} \)
  3. \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{1-x}{\sqrt{x-1}} \)
  4. \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x - \cos x + 1}{2x + \sin x} \)
  5. \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{x^4 - 2x^3 + x - 1}{x^4 - 3x + x^3 - 2} \)
  6. \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{\sin(x^2 + 1)}{x^2} \)
